Professional Summary

Surgical pathologist and scientist with subspecialty expertise in pulmonary, genitourinary, and cardiovascular pathology. Founder and CEO of Argos Laboratory, a reference anatomic and molecular pathology laboratory in Fortaleza, Brazil. Professor in the Graduate Program in Pathology at the Federal University of Ceará. Contributor to the WHO Classification of Tumours and member of the IASLC Pathology Committee.
