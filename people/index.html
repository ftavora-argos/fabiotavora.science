Research Group

Current and former members of the Távora Research Group at the Federal University of Ceará Graduate Program in Pathology and Argos Laboratory.

Principal Investigator

Fabio Távora, MD, PhD Professor, Federal University of Ceará · CEO, Argos Laboratory

Surgical Pathologist with subspecialty expertise in pulmonary, genitourinary, and cardiovascular pathology. Post-doctoral fellow at Brown University (2019–2020). Contributor to the WHO Classification of Tumours (Lung/Thymus/Heart 2014, 2021, 2025–26; GU 2021) and member of the IASLC Pathology Committee (2025–2027).

ORCID · Google Scholar

Cleto Dantas Nogueira, MD PhD Candidate · Federal University of Ceará

Selected publications: HER2-mutated NSCLC in Northeastern Brazil (Oncotarget, 2025) · GSK-3β and overall survival in NSCLC (Front Oncol, 2021) · Molecular profile of NSCLC (J Bras Pneumol, 2019)

Technical and Quality Manager at Argos Laboratory. Research focus: biomarker expression in lung cancer (GSK-3β, PD-L1) and digital pathology applications including QuPath-based quantification and urothelial carcinoma grading.

Affiliated with the Department of Pathology at UFC and UNIFOR. Research focus: impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on lung cancer diagnosis, incidental pulmonary nodule detection, and immune profiling of NSCLC. Completed PhD thesis in 2025 on COVID-19’s impact on lung neoplasm diagnosis in Ceará.

Selected publications: Immune profiling of incidental NSCLC patients (Appl Immunohistochem, 2025) · Impact of COVID-19 pandemic on lung cancer diagnosis (J Bras Pneumol, 2022)

André Costa Teixeira, MD, PhD Research Collaborator · Argos Laboratory · UNICHRISTUS

Doctor in Medical Sciences (UFC), MSc in Pathology (UFC). Pathologist at Argos Laboratory and Professor of Human Pathology at UNICHRISTUS. Residency at Hospital das Clínicas da Faculdade de Medicina de Ribeirão Preto–USP, with international exchange at the University of Porto, Portugal. Research focus: renal pathology, transplant pathology, hepatocellular carcinoma, and endothelial biomarkers in kidney allografts.
