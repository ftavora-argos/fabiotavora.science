Founded in 2011, **Argos Laboratory** is a regional reference center for surgical and molecular pathology, with subspecialty expertise in oncologic, pulmonary, genitourinary, and cardiovascular pathology.

**Key activities:**
- Surgical pathology of over 100,000 specimens annually
- Molecular diagnostics including EGFR, ALK, PD-L1, HER2, and MSI testing
- Integration of digital pathology and AI-assisted image analysis workflows
- Quality assurance and laboratory accreditation
- Multidisciplinary tumor board participation (thoracic, breast, genitourinary)
