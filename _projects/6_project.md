At Argos Laboratory, we are actively developing and deploying **AI-assisted diagnostic tools** to improve accuracy, efficiency, and reproducibility in surgical pathology.

**Current initiatives:**

- AI-powered immunohistochemistry quantification (PD-L1, Ki-67, HER2)
- Digital slide triage and navigation for high-volume oncologic cases
- Pattern recognition models for lung cancer subtype classification
- Cell quantification pipelines for immune profiling
- Collaboration with software engineers on custom pathology AI tools
- Digital cytopathology — comparative studies of glass vs. digital slides for cervical cytology

**Related publication:**
- Velozo et al., *Diagnostic Cytopathology* 2023 — Comparison of glass and digital slides for cervical cytopathology screening
