During my time at the **Armed Forces Institute of Pathology** and the **University of Maryland**, I developed deep expertise in cardiovascular pathology, resulting in two major textbooks and a large body of peer-reviewed work.

**Books:**
- *Practical Cardiovascular Pathology* — Burke & Távora, Lippincott Williams & Wilkins, 2012
- *Atlas of Tumor Pathology: Tumors of the Heart and Great Vessels* — Burke, Távora, Maleszewski & Frazier, AFIP, 2014

**Research areas:**
- Arrhythmogenic right ventricular cardiomyopathy (ARVC) — desmosomal protein mutations and autopsy findings
- Sudden cardiac death — morphologic substrates, coronary artery disease, hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
- Cardiac sarcoidosis
- Aortitis and aortic aneurysms
- Cardiac allograft pathology and transplant rejection
- Cardiac tumors — classification and WHO contributions
- Chagas disease cardiomyopathy

**Selected publications:**
- Maleszewski et al., *J Thorac Oncol* 2022
- Burke & Távora, *J Thorac Oncol* 2016
- Zhang et al., *Am J Surg Pathol* 2012
- Távora et al., *Human Pathology* 2012
