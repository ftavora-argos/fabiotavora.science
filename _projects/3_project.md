I have been an invited contributor to the **WHO Classification of Tumours** across multiple organ systems and editions, participating in editorial and consensus conferences that define the international standard for tumor classification.

**Contributions:**

- WHO Classification of Tumours of the Lung, Thymus and Heart — **2014 edition** (Lyon, France)
- WHO Classification of Tumours of the Lung, Thymus and Heart — **2021 edition** (online)
- WHO Classification of Tumours of the Genitourinary Tract — **2021 edition** (online)
- WHO Classification of Tumours of the Lung, Thymus and Heart — **2025–2026 edition** (current member)

**Related publications:**
- Maleszewski et al., *J Thorac Oncol* 2022 — The 2021 WHO Classification of Tumors of the Heart
- Burke & Távora, *J Thorac Oncol* 2016 — The 2015 WHO Classification of Tumors of the Heart and Pericardium
