## Principal Investigator

---

**Fabio Távora, MD, PhD**
*Professor, Federal University of Ceará · CEO, Argos Laboratory*

Surgical Pathologist with subspecialty expertise in pulmonary, genitourinary, and cardiovascular pathology. Post-doctoral fellow at Brown University (2019–2020). Contributor to the WHO Classification of Tumours (Lung/Thymus/Heart 2014, 2021, 2025–26; GU 2021) and member of the IASLC Pathology Committee (2025–2027).

[ORCID](https://orcid.org/0000-0003-2361-0613) · [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=7d50ByEAAAAJ)
